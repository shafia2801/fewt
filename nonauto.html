Types of Clouds

Clouds are visible masses of water droplets or ice crystals suspended in the Earth's atmosphere. They come in various forms and are classified into several primary types:

Cumulus Clouds

Cumulus clouds are fluffy, white clouds with a puffy appearance.

Stratus Clouds

Stratus clouds are low, flat clouds that cover the sky like a blanket.

Cirrus Clouds

Cirrus clouds are thin and wispy clouds that occur at high altitudes.
